Top 5 Ultrasonic Testing (UT) Mistakes in 2025

By: S. Mousavi • Senior Inspection Engineer • 16+ Years in Oil & Gas

  1. Ignoring surface condition before scanning — leads to false calls.
  2. Using wrong probe angle for weld type (e.g., 70° for butt weld).
  3. Not calibrating on actual material thickness — skews DAC curves.
  4. Missing lamination in plate materials — critical for pressure vessels.
  5. Poor reporting without waveform screenshots — not API 510 compliant.
